About Me

Building from Haiti, for Haiti.

I am Pete Philentrope — entrepreneur, AI professional, and community builder based in Port-au-Prince, Haiti. I work at the intersection of technology, financial inclusion, and social impact.

I founded HaitiCard to give 11 million Haitians access to virtual cards and global digital payments. I founded FatherLeader Foundation after my son Akim was sent home from school because I fell behind on his school balance — and I decided no other parent should carry that alone.

I am also a trilingual content creator, AI data annotator, and remote operations professional with 5+ years of experience working with international clients across time zones.
